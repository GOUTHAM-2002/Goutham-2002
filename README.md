# 💫 About Me:
🔭 I'm currently working on **Mechanistic Interpretability and Cognitive Science applied to LLMs** — studying whether transformer models exhibit dual-process cognitive structure analogous to System 1/2 thinking.<br><br>🧠 My research sits at the intersection of **cognitive science, mechanistic interpretability, and multi-agent AI systems** — with the long-term goal of closing the gap between how humans think and how AI systems actually work.<br><br>📄 I have two papers currently under review: one at **ICML** on cognitive mode dissociation in LLMs via activation topology, and one at **IJCAI** on responsibility attribution and strategic abstention in multi-agent LLM systems.<br><br>🏛️ MS Computer Science (AI Track) at **Dartmouth College**, Minds Machines and Society Lab, advised by Prof. Soroush Vosoughi. Supported by the **AMD AI Pervasive Research Scholarship**.<br><br>💬 Ask me about **mechanistic interpretability, distributed LLM training, multi-agent systems, or applying cog sci to AI**.<br><br>📫 How to reach me: **goutham3336@gmail.com**<br><br>⚡ Fun fact: The cognitive mode paper started from reading a book on dual-process theory and refusing to let go of the question of whether LLMs do the same thing.
